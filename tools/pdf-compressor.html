Free Online PDF Compressor – No Watermark

Need to email a large PDF or upload it to a website with a size limit? Our Simple PDF Compressor helps you reduce your PDF file sizes quickly. This basic compressor works by re-rendering embedded images at a lower resolution, which is one of the most effective ways to shrink PDFs.

For best results, this tool is ideal for PDFs containing images, scans, or photos. Text-only PDFs are already quite compact and may not see significant size reduction.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this PDF compressor work?
It reads the PDF, extracts embedded images, re-renders them at a reduced quality, and repackages the result. This is a simple, client-side approach ideal for image-heavy PDFs.
Will I lose text quality?
No. Text elements remain intact. Only embedded images are recompressed.
Is it safe to use?
Absolutely. Your file is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is ever uploaded.
