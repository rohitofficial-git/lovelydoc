Why Use a PDF Instead of Multiple Images?

1. Consistency: Pictures can look different on various screen sizes, but a PDF maintains a fixed layout.

2. Easier Sharing: Instead of sending 20 separate JPG files, you can send one single PDF file.

3. Security: PDFs are harder to edit accidentally than individual image files.
