Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

At LovelyDoc, your privacy is our top priority. All file processing on our platform happens entirely within your web browser. We do not upload, store, or transmit any of your files to our servers or any third-party service.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data, file data, or usage analytics. Our tools are fully client-side, meaning all operations run locally on your device.

Cookies

LovelyDoc does not use cookies for tracking purposes. If any cookies are used, they are strictly for improving user experience (e.g., remembering a rating preference) and contain no personal information.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party CDN services to deliver JavaScript libraries (such as jsPDF and pdf-lib). These CDNs may log basic access information as per their own privacy policies. No file data is ever shared with these services.

File Security

Your files are processed entirely in your browser's memory. They are never uploaded to any server. Once you close or refresh the page, all processed data is discarded from memory.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at toolsforusofficial@gmail.com.
